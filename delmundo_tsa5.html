Tokyo

Robber

Silene Oliveira, better known by her code-name Tokyo ( Spanish: Tokio ), was one of the main protagonists of the Netflix series Money Heist, as well as the narrator of the story. She was a member of the heist crew set to rob the Royal Mint of Spain.

Rio

Robber

Rio was born in Getafe. His nickname given by his mother was Rayo, meaning lightning bolt in Spanish. At young age he became interested in computers and began programming at the age of 6.

Berlin

Robber

Berlin (Pedro Alonso) is arguably the most fascinating character from the Money Heist gang. In the original series, he's a terminally ill grand larcenist who comes off as a psychopath and an egocentric narcissist, but someone who also values the codes of friendship and would eventually sacrifice himself for it.

El Professor

Mastermind

Sergio Marquina, better known as The Professor, is the main protagonist of the Netflix series Money Heist. He is the mastermind behind the Royal Mint of Spain Heist and the Bank of Spain Heist. The Professor is a cunning individual that plans ahead and will make sure that everything goes according to plan.

Denver

Robber

Denver was raised by his father Moscow, who abandoned Denver's mother at a roundabout due to her drug use. For many years, Moscow led Denver to believe that his mother abandoned him.
